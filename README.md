# Integrated Three-Statement Financial Model in Excel

## Project Overview

This project is a fully integrated Three-Statement Financial Model built in Microsoft Excel. The model connects the Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement through supporting schedules and forecast assumptions to analyze a company's historical performance and project future financial results.

The purpose of this project was to strengthen my financial modeling, forecasting, and Excel skills while developing a deeper understanding of how the three core financial statements interact.

---

## Model Components

### Income Statement

* Revenue forecasting
* Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
* Gross Profit
* Operating Expenses
* EBITDA
* Depreciation & Amortization
* Interest Expense
* Pre-Tax Income
* Net Income

### Balance Sheet

* Cash
* Trade Receivables
* Inventory
* Property, Plant & Equipment (PP&E)
* Other Assets
* Debt
* Trade Payables
* Other Liabilities
* Shareholders' Equity

### Cash Flow Statement

* Cash Flow from Operations
* Cash Flow from Investing Activities
* Cash Flow from Financing Activities
* Net Change in Cash
* Ending Cash Balance
